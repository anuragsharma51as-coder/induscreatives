FAQ

Before you write in…

The answers to the four questions we get most often.

  • Q · 01

    How quickly can you start?

    Most Discover phases kick off within 1–2 weeks of signing. Build phases start the week after Discover closes.

  • Q · 02

    Do you work with international clients?

    Yes. Roughly half of our engagements are outside India. We bill in USD, EUR, or GBP; overlap hours for US Pacific to UK time zones are easy to arrange.

  • Q · 03

    Who owns the code?

    You do — from the first commit. Repos are in your GitHub/GitLab org, not ours. Full IP assignment is standard in our MSA.

  • Q · 04

    Will you sign a BAA / NDA?

    Absolutely. BAAs for healthcare work, NDAs for anything sensitive — we have standard templates, or we can paper against yours.
