01 · Technical Skills

Four disciplines, held close.

Every engagement at Indus Creatives draws from the same four core disciplines. We don’t outsource these; we practise them.

TS · 01

UI/UX Prototyping

From low-fidelity sketches to clickable Figma prototypes. We prototype before we build — to save you the most expensive line of code there is: the one you write twice.

TS · 02

Data Modeling

Clean, normalised schemas and well-reasoned indexes. Event-driven data pipelines, warehouse modelling, and the boring plumbing that makes everything upstream feel fast.

TS · 03

QA & Testing

Manual exploratory testing for the human feel, plus automated unit, integration, and E2E suites for the repeatable stuff. We ship with regression safety nets, not wishful thinking.

TS · 04

AI/ML Model Deployment

Training is the easy half. We handle the rest: packaging, versioning, deploying, monitoring, drift detection, cost controls, and rollback. Models that behave in production — not just in notebooks.
