Indus Creatives is a small, deliberate studio. We borrow our silhouette from an archway: two pillars, one commanding opening, a quiet signature on top. In practice, that means software built to stand — thoughtful, durable, and earned line by line.

01 · The Bench

A collaborative bench of designers, developers, and business analysts — assembled for your brief.

Every project is led by a dedicated developer, supported by a curated team drawn from our network. You get named owners, weekly demos, and a runbook you can hand to the next engineer on day one.

Designers frame the problem. Developers carry the load. Business analysts make sure the result actually maps to how you run the company. Three disciplines — one bench.

  • 01

  • 02

  • 03

    Business-Aware Delivery

    We understand P&Ls, SLAs, and the realities of a ten-person ops team. Software follows.

  • 04

    Post-Launch Care

02 · The Process

How an engagement actually runs.

We work in three phases. Every phase ends with a demo, a decision, and documented next steps. No mystery, no vanishing month.

Phase · 01

Discover

Two weeks to frame the problem. Stakeholder interviews, existing-system audit, brand and technical constraints, measurable goals. Output: a scope, a roadmap, and a pricing range we both believe in.

Phase · 02

Build

Design and engineering in weekly sprints. Demos every Friday. A shared Notion or Linear board. Code in a repo you own from day one. MVP milestone at the midpoint.

Phase · 03

Ship & Tend

Launch, analytics instrumentation, runbook, and a structured 30-60-90 day care window. Optional ongoing retainer for iteration, security patches, and new surface area.

03 · Engagements

Three ways to begin with us.

Most engagements fall into one of three shapes. Pick the one closest to where you are — we can always reshape in Discover.

Engagement · A

Idea → MVP

Zero-to-one build for founders and intrapreneurs. We compress the usual six-month MVP into a disciplined 10–14 week arc. Design, build, launch, measure — with a clear decision point at the end on whether to scale.

Engagement · B

App Revitalisation

For products that work but have stopped ageing well. We stabilise the codebase, retire the brittle parts, re-platform where it pays back, and leave behind documentation the next developer can actually read.

Engagement · C

Strategic AI Consultation

For teams asking “should we be using AI for this?” We run a focused workshop, survey your workflows, flag the two or three places a model actually earns its keep, and scope a realistic pilot.

Engagement · D

Ongoing Studio Retainer

A monthly commitment that gives you access to the bench — design, development, QA, and AI/ML capacity — without hiring a full in-house team. Fixed scope each month, re-planned each quarter.
